Circular Charts

Description:

  • 1. Accidents by Emirate (Pie Chart): This visualization offers a pie chart breakdown of the total number of accidents by Emirate. Users can interact with the pie chart by clicking on a specific Emirate segment. This interaction filters the other visualizations to reflect data pertinent to the selected Emirate.
  • 2. Accident Types in Selected Emirate (Nested Pie Chart): This visualization tracks the breakdown of different types of accidents within a selected Emirate using a nested pie chart. Like the first visualization, clicking on a segment allows users to focus on specific accident types, influencing data shown in other components.
  • 3. Accident Trends Over Time (Line Chart): A time trend line chart displays changes in accident counts over time, segmented by month and filtered by selected Emirates or accident types. This chart allows users to view trends over time and identify patterns.

Pie and Stacked Bar

Description:
