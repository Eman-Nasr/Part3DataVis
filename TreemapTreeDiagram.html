Description:

1st visualization: tree diagram provides a hierarchical overview of geographic regions, starting with the "United Arab Emirates" at the top level and branching down into emirates, then areas. It visualizes the structure in a tidy or cluster format, allowing users to explore relationships and hierarchical dependencies. 2nd Visualization: treemap illustrates the proportional distribution of accidents across different emirates and areas. Each rectangle's size represents the scaled count of accidents, providing an intuitive view of where incidents are concentrated. The hierarchical nesting reflects the emirates and their respective areas. Interaction: Clicking on a node in the tree or a rectangle in the treemap highlights the selected region, enabling a focused view of the chosen emirate or area.
